What is the AmaClone Affiliate Program?

The AmaClone Affiliate Program is a way for website owners, bloggers, influencers, and content creators to earn commissions by promoting AmaClone products and driving sales through their platforms.

When you join our affiliate program, you'll get access to millions of products available on AmaClone.in. Simply create links to the products you want to promote, and when customers click through your links and make purchases, you earn commissions on qualifying sales.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the AmaClone Affiliate Program?

Anyone with a website, blog, app, or social media presence can join our affiliate program. You must be at least 18 years old and have a valid bank account in India to receive payments.

How much can I earn as an affiliate?

Earnings vary based on your traffic, the products you promote, and your audience's purchasing habits. Our top affiliates earn lakhs of rupees per month, while beginners might start with a few thousand rupees.

When and how will I get paid?

Payments are made monthly for earnings that exceed the minimum threshold of ₹250. We pay directly to your bank account through electronic transfer, typically within 60 days after the end of the month in which the commissions were earned.

What is the cookie duration?

When a visitor clicks on your affiliate link, a 24-hour cookie is set. If the visitor makes a qualifying purchase within that 24-hour window, you earn a commission on that sale.

Are there any restrictions on how I can promote AmaClone products?

Yes, there are guidelines you must follow. For example, you cannot use paid search ads with AmaClone trademarks, send spam emails, or make false claims about products. Please review our complete program policies for details.
