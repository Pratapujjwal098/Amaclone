Company Overview

About AmaClone India

AmaClone India Private Limited is one of India's leading e-commerce companies, providing millions of customers with access to over 100 million products across dozens of categories. Founded in 2015, our mission is to be India's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online at the best possible prices.

Today, AmaClone India operates as a subsidiary of AmaClone Global, employing over 10,000 people across offices, fulfillment centers, and customer service centers throughout India. We are headquartered in Bengaluru, with regional offices in Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Chennai.

Corporate Governance

Governance Structure

At AmaClone India, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance to ensure accountability, transparency, and ethical business practices. Our governance framework is designed to promote responsible decision-making that balances the interests of all stakeholders, including customers, employees, partners, and shareholders.

Our Board of Directors provides oversight and guidance to the executive team, ensuring that our business strategies align with our long-term goals and values. The Board includes both executive and independent directors with diverse expertise across e-commerce, technology, finance, and business management.
