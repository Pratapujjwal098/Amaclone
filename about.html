About AmaClone

Connecting millions of customers with quality products at the best prices since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, AmaClone India started with a simple mission: to create the most customer-centric online marketplace in India, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online at the best possible prices.

What began as a modest online bookstore has transformed into India's largest e-commerce platform, offering millions of products across dozens of categories. Today, we serve customers across the entire Indian subcontinent, delivering products to even the most remote areas.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction, innovation, and our core values has helped us grow into one of the most trusted and beloved brands in India. Every day, we work to earn and keep our customers' trust by focusing on their needs and continuously improving our services.
