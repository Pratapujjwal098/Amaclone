Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

To track your order:

  1. Sign in to your AmaClone account
  2. Go to "Your Orders"
  3. Find the order you want to track and click "Track Package"
  4. View the delivery status and estimated arrival date

You can also track your order directly from the shipping confirmation email by clicking the "Track your package" button.

How do I return an item?

To return an item:

  1. Sign in to your AmaClone account
  2. Go to "Your Orders"
  3. Select the order with the item you want to return
  4. Click "Return items"
  5. Select the item(s) and reason for return
  6. Choose your preferred return method and print the return label
  7. Package the item securely and attach the return label
  8. Drop off the package at the specified location

Most items can be returned within 30 days of receipt.

When will I receive my refund?

After we receive your return:

  • Credit card refunds: 2-3 business days to process, 5-7 business days to appear on your statement
  • Debit card refunds: 2-3 business days to process, 5-10 business days to appear in your account
  • UPI refunds: 1-2 business days to process
  • Bank account refunds: 5-7 business days to process
  • AmaClone Pay Balance: Refunded immediately after the return is processed

You can check the status of your refund in "Your Orders" under the "Order Details" page.

How do I cancel an order?

To cancel an order:

  1. Sign in to your AmaClone account
  2. Go to "Your Orders"
  3. Find the order you want to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Items"
  5. Select the items you want to cancel and the reason
  6. Click "Cancel selected items"

Orders can only be cancelled before they enter the shipping process. If your order has already shipped, you'll need to wait for it to arrive and then return it.

What are the benefits of AmaClone Prime?

AmaClone Prime membership includes:

  • Unlimited free delivery on eligible items with no minimum order value
  • Free same-day and one-day delivery in select areas
  • Early access to deals and lightning deals
  • Unlimited access to award-winning movies and TV shows with Prime Video
  • Ad-free music with Prime Music
  • Free in-game content and free games with Prime Gaming
  • Unlimited 5% cashback on all purchases using AmaClone Pay ICICI Bank credit card
  • Exclusive deals and discounts for Prime members

Prime membership costs ₹1,499 per year or ₹179 per month.

How do I report a problem with my order?

If you have issues with your order:

  1. Sign in to your AmaClone account
  2. Go to "Your Orders"
  3. Find the problematic order
  4. Click "Problem with order"
  5. Select the specific issue from the options provided
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to resolve your issue

If you need additional assistance, contact our customer service through the "Contact Us" page.
